Welcome to VAMNICOM

The National Institute is headed by Dr. Hema Yadav, Director. The Academic strength of the Institute lies in a team of highly qualified and dedicated core faculty comprising of six Professors, eight Associate Professors, five Asstt. Professors, four Lecturers. Most of the faculty members have vast experience both at the university and National level Institutions on one hand as well as industry experience on the other. They have had international exposure in the area of their specialization. The faculty members have the distinctive advantage of having intellectual interactions with delegates around .
