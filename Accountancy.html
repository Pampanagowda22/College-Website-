An accountancy course is designed to equip students with a strong foundation in financial accounting, managerial accounting, and auditing principles. The curriculum covers topics such as financial statements, budgeting, taxation, and internal controls. Students engage in practical exercises, case studies, and financial analysis to develop skills in financial reporting and decision-making. The program emphasizes ethical considerations in accounting practices and prepares students for careers in auditing, financial management, and consulting. Throughout the course, students gain a comprehensive understanding of accounting principles and their applications in various business contexts.
