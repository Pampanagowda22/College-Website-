An English course is designed to enhance students' language proficiency, communication skills, and critical analysis of literature. The curriculum covers various aspects of English, including grammar, literature, writing, and linguistic studies. Students engage in reading classic and contemporary works, writing essays and reports, and participating in discussions to develop their language and analytical abilities. The program emphasizes effective communication, literary analysis, and creative expression. Throughout the course, students are encouraged to explore diverse genres, periods, and cultural contexts within the English language.
